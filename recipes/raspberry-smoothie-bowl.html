Lovely to look at and fun to eat, this Raspberry Smoothie Bowl is packed with flavor and nutrition!Have fun garnishing your smoothie bowl with your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen raspberries
  • 1/2 peeled and cooked beet
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • Assorted berries
  • chopped roasted almonds
  • granola

Steps

  1. Place the raspberries,beet and almond milk in a blender. Blend until smooth and frothy
  2. Pour the smoothie into a bowl.
  3. Top with berries, almonds, and granola if desired.
